Our History

The SwiftLine Journey

Follow our path from a local delivery startup into a modern global transport titan.

2011

The Beginning

Founded as a small local courier service with a fleet of just 5 delivery vans in the city center.

2015

National Expansion

Expanded our network nationally and integrated automated sorting warehouses to drastically improve delivery times.

2019

Ocean & Air Freight

Pioneered our first international routes, forming strategic alliances with major global airlines and shipping ports.

2026

Global Dominance & Green Tech

Currently serving over 150 countries globally, utilizing AI trace routing and transitioning rapidly toward a fully green fleet.
