Got Questions?

Services FAQ

Common questions concerning our specific transit operations.

Is heavy machinery transport included in Ocean Freight?

Yes, we specialize in high-load heavy machinery handling, particularly in our Ocean and specialized Road transit tiers. Custom cranes and out-of-gauge load logistics are available.

What happens if a package is delayed in customs?

Our dedicated customs brokerage operates strictly to proactively clear goods before they land. In the rare event of a flag, our agents work 24/7 on-site to release the shipment immediately.

Can you warehouse my products temporarily before delivery?

Absolutely. We operate a massive smart-storage network. We can hold bulk cargo indefinitely, and dispatch it in smaller packages as your e-commerce demands rise.

Does SwiftLine provide comprehensive cargo insurance?

All basic routes come with standard carrier liability. Additionally, we offer optional zero-deductible Premium Freight Insurance covering 100% of declared value across all transport modes.

Unsure which service fits best?

Talk to our transport advisors today. We'll map out a personalized logistical pipeline for your products perfectly matched to your budget.
